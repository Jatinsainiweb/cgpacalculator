Convert CGPA to Percentage Instantly

Use our CGPA to Percentage Calculator to quickly convert your Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) into percentage marks. This tool is designed for Indian university students and supports popular institutions like VIT, SRM, AKTU, LPU, and more.

CGPA to Percentage Conversion Formula

The most common formula used by Indian universities to convert CGPA to percentage is:

UniversityFormula
General/CBSEPercentage = CGPA × 9.5
VITPercentage = (CGPA - 0.5) × 10
SRMPercentage = (CGPA - 0.5) × 10
AKTUPercentage = (CGPA - 0.75) × 10
LPUPercentage = (CGPA - 0.5) × 10

Always check your university’s official conversion rule for accuracy.

Example Conversion

Suppose your CGPA is 8.2 and you are from VIT:

  • Formula: (CGPA - 0.5) × 10
  • Calculation: (8.2 - 0.5) × 10 = 7.7 × 10 = 77%
