Understanding CGPA to Percentage Conversion

Different universities in India use different formulas to convert CGPA to percentage. Most commonly:

Percentage = CGPA × Multiplication Factor

Where Multiplication Factor is either 9.5 or 10

Example Calculations

For CGPA = 8.75:

  • Using 9.5 factor: 8.75 × 9.5 = 83.13%
  • Using 10 factor: 8.75 × 10 = 87.50%

Important Notes

  • Different universities use different conversion factors
  • Most private universities use 9.5 as multiplier
  • Most government universities use 10 as multiplier
  • Some universities may have custom formulas
  • Always verify the formula with your university
