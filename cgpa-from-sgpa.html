How to Calculate CGPA from SGPA – Step-by-Step

  1. List all your SGPAs for each semester completed.
  2. Add up all the SGPA values.
  3. Count the total number of semesters.
  4. Divide the total SGPA by the number of semesters.
  5. The result is your CGPA.

CGPA Calculation Formula from SGPA

The general formula for calculating CGPA from SGPA is:

CGPA = (Sum of SGPAs in all semesters) / (Total Number of Semesters)

Some universities may use credit-weighted averages. Always check your university’s guidelines.

Example Calculation

Suppose your SGPAs for 6 semesters are 8.1, 8.3, 8.0, 8.5, 8.2, and 8.4:

  • Total SGPA = 8.1 + 8.3 + 8.0 + 8.5 + 8.2 + 8.4 = 49.5
  • Number of Semesters = 6
  • CGPA = 49.5 / 6 = 8.25

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is SGPA?

SGPA stands for Semester Grade Point Average. It is the average grade point earned in a particular semester.

2. How do I calculate CGPA from SGPA?

Add all your SGPAs and divide by the number of semesters completed.

3. Can I use decimal SGPA values?

Yes, decimal SGPA values (e.g., 8.25) are common and accepted.

4. Is CGPA the same as percentage?

No, CGPA is a grade point average. To convert to percentage, use your university’s conversion formula (e.g., CGPA × 9.5).
