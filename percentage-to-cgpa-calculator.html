Percentage to CGPA Conversion Formula

The most common formula used by Indian universities to convert percentage to CGPA is:

UniversityFormula
General/CBSECGPA = Percentage / 9.5
VITCGPA = (Percentage / 10) + 0.5
SRMCGPA = (Percentage / 10) + 0.5
AKTUCGPA = (Percentage / 10) + 0.75
LPUCGPA = (Percentage / 10) + 0.5

Always check your university’s official conversion rule for accuracy.
