Introduction

Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), headquartered in Belgaum, Karnataka, is one of India's largest technical universities, governing over 200+ engineering colleges. VTU follows a Grade Point Average (GPA) system to evaluate student performance each semester. Calculating GPA helps students track academic progress, meet placement eligibility, and apply for higher studies like MTech or MBA.

Why Calculate GPA?

  • Placements: Companies like Infosys and Wipro set GPA cutoffs (e.g., 7.0+).
  • Higher Education: Universities require GPA for admissions.
  • Backlog Avoidance: Monitor grades to ensure timely graduation.

VTU GPA Calculation Formula

Official Formula

GPA = Σ (Credit × Grade Point) ÷ Total Credits

Combining Internal & External Marks

  • Internal (IA): 20–30 marks (varies by subject).
  • External (ESA): 70–80 marks.
  • Total Marks: IA + ESA.

Example:

If you score 25/30 in IA and 60/70 in ESA, total marks = 85/100 → Grade A (9 points).

FAQs

1. What is GPA in VTU out of?
