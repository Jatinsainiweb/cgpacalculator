About VTU GPA Calculation

Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) uses a specific grading system to evaluate student performance. The Grade Point Average (GPA) is calculated based on the marks obtained in each subject, weighted by the credit points assigned to that subject.

The formula for calculating GPA in VTU is:

GPA = Σ(Grade Points × Credits) / Σ(Credits)

For example, if you scored 85 marks in a 4-credit subject, you would receive 9 grade points (A grade). This would contribute 9 × 4 = 36 grade points to your total.

Important Notes:

  • VTU typically assigns different credit weights to theory subjects, laboratories, and projects
  • A minimum of 40% marks is required to pass a subject
  • This calculator follows the official VTU grading system as per university guidelines
  • The semester GPA (SGPA) calculation does not include failed subjects
