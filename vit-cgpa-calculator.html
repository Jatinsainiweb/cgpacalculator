VIT Grading System

VIT follows a 10-point grading system:

  • S (Superior): 10
  • A (Excellent): 9
  • B (Very Good): 8
  • C (Good): 7
  • D (Average): 6
  • E (Poor): 5
  • F (Fail): 0

VIT Formula: Percentage = CGPA × 9.5
