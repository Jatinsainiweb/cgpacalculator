Convert SGPA to CGPA Instantly

SGPA to CGPA Conversion Formula

The most common formula used by Indian universities to convert SGPA to CGPA is:

StepDescription
1Add all SGPAs obtained in each semester.
2Divide the total SGPA by the number of semesters.
3Result is your CGPA.

Some universities may have specific rules, so always check your university’s official guidelines.

Example Conversion

Suppose your SGPAs for 4 semesters are 8.0, 8.2, 7.9, and 8.5:

  • Total SGPA = 8.0 + 8.2 + 7.9 + 8.5 = 32.6
  • Number of Semesters = 4
  • CGPA = 32.6 / 4 = 8.15
