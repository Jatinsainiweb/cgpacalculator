JNU Grading System

JNU follows a 10-point grading system:

  • A+ (Exceptional): 10
  • A (Excellent): 9
  • B+ (Very Good): 8
  • B (Good): 7
  • C+ (Fair): 6
  • C (Average): 5
  • D (Pass): 4
  • F (Fail): 0

JNU Formula: Percentage = CGPA × 10
