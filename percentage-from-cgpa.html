How to Calculate Percentage from CGPA – Step-by-Step

  1. Find your CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) from your marksheet or calculate it using our CGPA calculator.
  2. Check your university’s official conversion formula (most use CGPA × 9.5).
  3. Multiply your CGPA by the conversion factor to get your percentage.
UniversityFormula
General/CBSEPercentage = CGPA × 9.5
VITPercentage = (CGPA - 0.5) × 10
SRMPercentage = (CGPA - 0.5) × 10
AKTUPercentage = (CGPA - 0.75) × 10
LPUPercentage = (CGPA - 0.5) × 10

Always check your university’s official conversion rule for accuracy.

CGPA to Percentage Calculator

Example Calculation

Suppose your CGPA is 8.2 and you are from VIT:

  • Formula: (CGPA - 0.5) × 10
  • Calculation: (8.2 - 0.5) × 10 = 7.7 × 10 = 77%

For CBSE/General: 8.2 × 9.5 = 77.9%
