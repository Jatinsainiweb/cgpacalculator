About Us

Simplifying Academic Grade Calculations

Our Story

CGPA Calculator was born out of necessity. As students ourselves, we experienced the challenges of manually calculating grades, converting between different grading systems, and trying to forecast academic performance. We found that many students struggled with the same issues – spending hours on tedious calculations or being unsure about how to properly weight courses with different credit values.

In 2020, we decided to create a simple, user-friendly solution. What started as a small project quickly grew as more students discovered and shared our tools. Today, CGPA Calculator serves thousands of students across India and beyond, helping them accurately track their academic performance and set meaningful goals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students with accessible, accurate tools for academic planning and assessment. We believe that understanding your academic performance should be straightforward and stress-free, allowing you to focus on what truly matters – learning and growth.
