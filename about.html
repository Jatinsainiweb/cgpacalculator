About Us

Empowering Students with Accurate Grade Calculations

Our Mission

At CGPA Calculator, our mission is to provide students with accurate, reliable, and easy-to-use tools for calculating their academic performance metrics. We understand the importance of precise grade calculations in academic planning and career development.

Our Vision

We envision being the most trusted academic calculator platform for Indian students, helping them make informed decisions about their education and future careers through accurate grade calculations and conversions.

Our Story

Started in 2025, CGPA Calculator was born from the need for a reliable and accurate grade calculation system for Indian university students. What began as a simple calculator has grown into a comprehensive platform serving students across India.

Our team consists of education enthusiasts and tech experts who understand the Indian education system and its various grading methods. We continuously update our calculators to reflect the latest changes in university grading systems.
