DU Grading System

Delhi University follows a 10-point grading system:

  • O (Outstanding): 10
  • A+ (Excellent): 9
  • A (Very Good): 8
  • B+ (Good): 7
  • B (Above Average): 6
  • C (Average): 5
  • P (Pass): 4
  • F (Fail): 0

DU Formula: Percentage = CGPA × 9.5
